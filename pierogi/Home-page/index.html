About Me

My passion for cooking was born in my family home. I remember helping my grandmother prepare meals when I was a child. Over time, cooking brought me more and more joy and satisfaction. Pierogi are a very specific and time-consuming dish, but they are delicious. They are unique and one of a kind. As time went on, it turned out that my passion became my small family business. I love making pierogi with various fillings, from traditional ones to new flavor combinations.
